Q&A

How do I register?

Participants can register by filling out the registration form on the website. After submitting the form, you'll be redirected to join a whatsapp community to keep you updated on the proceedings

Who is eligible to participate?

The competition is open to students who meet the specified academic level stated in the competition guidelines

Is there a registration fee?

No, there won't be any form of fee attached to the registration

What kind of questions will be asked?

The competition focuses on mathematical reasoning, problem solving and logical thinking. Questions would be advanced calculus related

What are the prizes for winners?

Top participants receive awards such as certificates, medals, recognition on the competition's Hall of fame and cash prize

How do i get into the hall of fame?

Participants who achieve outstanding performance in the competition such as attaining a perfect score in any or all of the rounds of the context are featured in the hall of fame section
